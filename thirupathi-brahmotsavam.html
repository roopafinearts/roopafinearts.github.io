Thirupathi Brahmotsavam

Tirumala Brahmotsavam is one of the most important festivals celebrated in the Tirumala or Thirupathi (a city in Andhra Pradesh) for Lord Sri Venkateswara. During Brahmotsavam, the Lord holds court in the main temple, after being taken around in a procession.

In this display, you will see Lord Venkateshwara's vahanas for the Brahmotsavam. These are the different vahanas that he has taken around the procession.
